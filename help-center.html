Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started after subscribing? v

After your subscription is activated, you'll be guided through our quick setup wizard that helps you:

  • Set up your business information
  • Choose your brand colors and style
  • Configure your default pricing
  • Add your photography services

The entire process takes about 5 minutes and can be modified anytime from your settings.

How do I add my first photography session? v

To add a new session:

  1. Click the "Add New Session" button on your dashboard
  2. Fill in client details (name, email, phone)
  3. Select the session type and date/time
  4. Set the location and pricing
  5. Add any notes or special requirements
  6. Click "Add Session" to save
How do I connect Stripe for payments? v

To accept payments through the platform:

  1. Go to Settings → Payment Setup
  2. Click "Connect Stripe Account"
  3. Follow Stripe's onboarding process
  4. Once connected, you can send invoices and accept payments

Note: You'll need a Stripe account. It's free to create one at stripe.com

How do I create and share client galleries? v

To create a photo gallery:

  1. Open the session you want to add photos to
  2. Click "Upload Photos"
  3. Select and upload your edited photos
  4. Click "Gallery" to view the gallery
  5. Click "Copy URL" to get the shareable link
  6. Send the link to your client

Galleries are password-protected and can be customized with your branding.

How do I build my portfolio website? v

Our website builder makes it easy:

  1. Click "Website Builder" from your dashboard
  2. Choose from 20+ professional templates
  3. Customize colors, fonts, and layout
  4. Add your portfolio images
  5. Update your business information
  6. Publish to your custom domain or free subdomain

All websites are mobile-responsive and SEO-optimized.
