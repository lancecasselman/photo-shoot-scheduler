Frequently Asked Questions

After uploading photos to a session, go to the gallery section and click "Share Gallery". You'll get a secure link that you can send to your client via email or text message. Clients don't need an account to view their photos.

First, check that the gallery link is correct and hasn't expired. Try opening the link in a different browser or clearing browser cache. If problems persist, contact support with the session ID and client email for immediate assistance.

All photos are stored with bank-level security using encrypted cloud storage, SSL connections, and access controls. Photos are backed up across multiple data centers and only accessible through secure, authenticated sessions.
