Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience

This Cookie Policy explains how Photography Management System uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and provide a personalized experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality like security, authentication, and basic navigation.

Purpose: Login sessions, security, basic functionality

Duration: Session or up to 30 days

Can be disabled: No (required for the service to work)

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

Purpose: User preferences, settings, customization

Duration: Up to 1 year

Can be disabled: Yes

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our platform so we can improve it. All analytics data is anonymized.

Purpose: Usage analytics, performance monitoring

Duration: Up to 2 years

Can be disabled: Yes

Specific Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session_id Maintains your login session 24 hours Essential
user_preferences Remembers your settings and preferences 1 year Functional
csrf_token Security protection against cross-site attacks Session Essential
analytics_id Anonymous usage tracking (if enabled) 2 years Analytics
theme_preference Remembers your dark/light mode choice 6 months Functional

Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase/Google: For authentication and secure login
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance optimization

These services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices, which we encourage you to review.

How to Manage Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Our Cookie Preferences

You can also manage your cookie preferences directly in your account settings. We provide granular controls for different types of cookies.

What Happens When You Disable Cookies

  • Essential cookies disabled: The platform won't work properly
  • Functional cookies disabled: You'll lose personalization features
  • Analytics cookies disabled: We can't improve the service based on usage data

Mobile App Considerations

Our mobile apps use similar technologies to cookies, such as:

  • Local storage: For offline functionality
  • Device identifiers: For authentication and security
  • App preferences: For personalization

You can manage these through your device's app settings.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our platform, we'll ask for your consent for non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

You can change your mind at any time through your account settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll notify you of significant changes and update the "Last Updated" date below.

Questions?

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@photomanagementsystem.com
